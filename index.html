About Us

Your Peaceful Escape in Keragala

Rajagaha Rest Garden Hotel is a peaceful and relaxing garden-style stay located in Keragala. Surrounded by lush greenery, it offers guests a calm and comfortable environment far away from city noise.

Ideal for travelers, couples, and families looking for a quiet, restorative experience in nature. Our garden setting provides the perfect backdrop for relaxation — whether you're sipping tea on the veranda or strolling through our green pathways.
